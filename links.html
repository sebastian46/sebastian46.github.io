However, hearing about a company who could easily pour millions into a project like this is not as exciting as the internet coming together to train this chess engine using their own resources. Leela knew no chess strategies at the beginning, and seeing her progress from the point where even a novice could beat her with ease, to beating the best open-source chess engine out, Stockfish, is something many patiently waited for.
